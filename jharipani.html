JHARIPANI WATERFALL

About

Jharipani fall is approx. 8 kms driving distance from Mussoorie and a mild two km hike will bring you to the majestic Jharipani falls. The passes through a gorgeous forest.
The walk through the pristine forest leads you to the Jharipani waterfall. Tourist stop and bath at the waterfall of Uttrakhand and even have a little picnic of our very own popular Maggi and fries in fact Tea/soft drinks. Exploring the world are some things we recommend and after the refreshing bath, a pleasant siesta is usually welcome. This hike is a simple and doable walk for all age groups.

Best time to go

The best time of the year to visit this secret waterfall is during summers to get some relief from the scroching heat of summers.
