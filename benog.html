B E N O G

About

Benog Wildlife Sanctuary is the best nature forest walk and day trek in the Mussoorie region. The forest walk to Benog Wildlife Sanctuary starts from Clouds End. This scenic nature trail starts as a jungle walk giving slendid forest views which further takes you to the Benog Hill peak which at 2,250 m is the highest mountain peak in Mussoorie region. It is suitable for people of all age groups & fitness level. Benog Hill is a must visit place for nature lovers, hikers, photographers and adventure seekers.
The Sanctuary serves as a home to many endangered and rare species of avifauna.Apart from fauna,herds of deer and leopards are also spotted in their natural habitat.Beautiful views of snow peaks like Chaukhamba and Bandar Punch can be seen from this Sanctuary.

Best time to go

You can visit benog Wildlife Sanctuary throughout the year to experience the Wildlife.
