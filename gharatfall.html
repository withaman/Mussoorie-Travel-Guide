GHARAT WATERFALL

About

A beatifull small waterfall near Banglo ki kandi village on the mainstream of kemptyfalls. Very popular amongst local travellers in search of peace and relax for a while.
On the side of river there are three old traditional water turbined flour mills (GHARAT) still in use by local people and villagers.

Best time to go

The best time of the year to visit this waterfall is throughout the year on weekends.
