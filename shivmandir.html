PRAKASHESHWAR MAHADEV

About

Shiv Mandir, also known as Shri Prakasheshwar Mahadev Temple, is a well-known Shiva temple in Dehradun located on the winding Mussoorie route.
Many Shiva devotees come to this shrine to ask for Mahadev's blessings. No offerings or donations are accepted at the temple, making it stand out from other temples in India.
On the grounds of the temple, pooja and artis are performed every day. On special occasions like Shivratri and Sawan, a "Bhandara" mass feast is organized, and prasad is given out to the attendees.
Shri Prakasheshwar Mahadev temple is privately owned and is around 1000 years old. Shiva's tridents can be seen on the temple's terrace, which talks of unusual architecture. The temple's walls are decorated with various shades of red, orange, and black paint.
