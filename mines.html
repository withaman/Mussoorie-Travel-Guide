LAMBI DEHAR MINES

About

A very old ruined mine of limestone which became populor amongst young travellers beacause of the less crowd ,view of the place and the peace here hits different.
Back in the 90's, some 50,000 mine workers died an agonizing death at the DEHAR MINES due to the faulty mine practices,due to that the mine was abandoned . Some of the local people also refer this place as haunted.

Best time to go

You can visit mines anytime of the year to explore then ruined place and enjoy the beauty of the place.
