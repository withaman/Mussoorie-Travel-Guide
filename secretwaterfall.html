SECRET WATERFALL

About

A majestic chilled waterfall on the river coming from the Benog Wildlife Sanctuary near Lakemist resort. A very short trail of about 10 min from the main road can lead you to this wonderfull hidden waterfall becoming more popular nowdays.

Best time to go

The best time of the year to visit this secret waterfall is during summers to get some relief from the scroching heat of summers.
