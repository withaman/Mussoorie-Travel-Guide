BANGLO KI KANDI

About

If you want to explore culture and something different than the rush of city life then you must visit the a small village named (Banglow ki Kandi).
Also know as the Tourist Village of Uttarakhand.
It lies in the foothills of Mussoorie and hill range of Benog Wildlife Sanctuary.
The village is surrounded by beautifull hills an a stream from one side . It is main stream of the kemptyfalls.
Thousands of tourist from the country of outside the country comes here to explore the local festivals, culture and the beauty of the village.

Best time to go

You can visit here anytime of the year to find peace and explore village but during a local festival known as (BHAND) a festival dedicated to nag devta and a kind of tug war,is the best time you must visit.
