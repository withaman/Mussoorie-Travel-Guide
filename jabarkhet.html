JABARKHET RESERVE

About

Jabarkhet Wildlife Reserve is a nature lover's paradise, nestled in the lower western Himalayas in the Indian state of Uttarakhand. Spread over 100 acres, the reserve is a perfect destination for adventure enthusiasts, bird watchers, nature photographers, and anyone who enjoys being in the midst of nature. #Trivia: It is the first wildlife sanctuary in Uttarakhand that is privately owned and run.
The Reserve boasts of a range of flora and fauna, including more than 50 species of birds, 150 species of plants, and a variety of mammals such as Himalayan Black Bear, Leopard, Barking Deer, and Langur. One of the most significant attractions of the Reserve is its Rhododendron forest, which is breathtakingly beautiful during the blooming season.
Jabarkhet Wildlife Reserve has a network of 8 well-marked trails that pass through streams, waterfalls, and forests, providing an immersive experience of the region's natural beauty. The Reserve also has a picnic area, a bird hide, and a small shop that sells locally produced goods.
Jabarkhet Wildlife Reserve is a hidden gem that has something for everyone.
